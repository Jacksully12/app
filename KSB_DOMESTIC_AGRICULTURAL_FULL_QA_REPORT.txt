SOURCE PRIORITY RULE
The 2026 Domestic booklet supersedes the 2025 Domestic chart whenever both contain the same domestic range. The 2025 chart is used to verify model separation, phase and technical columns; duplicating those rows would incorrectly double catalogue results.
