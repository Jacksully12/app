KSB FULL PDF RE-EXTRACTION QA REPORT - v4.9.0
================================================================

Source: Selection Chart_Domestic.pdf
Scope reviewed: PDF pages 5-44 / catalogue pages 1-40

Why this fix was needed
- Earlier extraction mixed KSB table columns in some borewell tables.
- Example issue: a head/flow value was being used as an ST stage suffix.
- Example wrong card: CORA 4AA + UMN (S) 100 50ST, 10 HP / 2 kW, Size 2.0 mm.

Parser changes
- Borewell pages are now parsed with explicit kW, HP, Stage, NRV size and flow/head columns.
- Tables with a separate Motor Type column are handled separately from oil-filled/RLX tables.
- KSB LPM, LPH and m3/hr units are detected and converted correctly to internal LPH.
- Surface/monobloc, booster, openwell, dewatering, sewage and KRTU/AMAREX tables were re-read from PDF tables.
