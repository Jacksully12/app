CROSS-BRAND COMPARISON QA REPORT v5.4.0
====================================================
- Comparison requires a specific normalized pump type.
- Texmo, Lubi and KSB are evaluated at the same head, flow and phase.
- Each brand shows one best match first and up to three alternatives.
- Ranking prioritizes tolerance band, closest duty-point difference, then connected power.
- Highlights identify closest duty-point match, lowest connected power and least oversupply.
- The app does not claim hydraulic efficiency without manufacturer efficiency data.
- Comparison can be copied or shared as one PNG image.
- Manual comparison uses the customer shortlist.
